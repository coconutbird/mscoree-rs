Add ICorDebug family interfaces for managed debugging

This adds the core debugging interfaces from mscordbi.dll:
- ICorDebug, ICorDebug2, ICorDebugController
- ICorDebugProcess through ICorDebugProcess11
- ICorDebugThread through ICorDebugThread4
- ICorDebugAppDomain through ICorDebugAppDomain4
- ICorDebugAssembly through ICorDebugAssembly3
- ICorDebugModule through ICorDebugModule4
- ICorDebugFunction through ICorDebugFunction4
- ICorDebugFrame, ICorDebugILFrame (1-4), ICorDebugNativeFrame
- ICorDebugValue (1-3), ICorDebugGenericValue, ICorDebugReferenceValue
- ICorDebugHeapValue (1-3)
- ICorDebugManagedCallback through ICorDebugManagedCallback4
- ICorDebugStepper (1-2)
- ICorDebugBreakpoint, ICorDebugFunctionBreakpoint, etc.
- ICorDebugEval (1-2)
- ICorDebugCode (1-4), ICorDebugILCode (1-2)
- ICorDebugChain, ICorDebugRegisterSet (1-2)
